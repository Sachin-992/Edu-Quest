# EDUCORE-OMEGA: Student Guide

> **Document Version**: 1.0  
> **Last Updated**: January 2026  
> **For**: Students

---

## Getting Started

### Your Login Credentials

- **Username**: Your email address (given by school)
- **Password**: Your date of birth in DDMMYYYY format

**Example:**
- Born on January 5, 2010
- Password: `05012010`

---

## Login Steps

1. Open the school app/website
2. Enter your email
3. Enter your date of birth as password (DDMMYYYY)
4. Click **Login**
5. You will see your Student Dashboard

---

## What You Can See

| Section | What's There |
|---------|--------------|
| **Overview** | Welcome message, today's date |
| **Attendance** | Your attendance record |
| **Marks** | Your exam scores |
| **Assignments** | Homework due dates |
| **Resources** | Study materials to download |

---

## View Your Attendance

1. Click **Attendance** tab
2. See calendar with:
   - ✓ Green = Present
   - ✗ Red = Absent
   - ⏰ Yellow = Late
3. See attendance percentage at top

---

## View Your Marks

1. Click **Marks** tab
2. See list of exams:
   - Subject
   - Exam type
   - Marks obtained
   - Maximum marks
   - Grade
3. Click on any exam to see details

---

## Download Study Materials

1. Click **Resources** tab
2. See list of files uploaded by teachers
3. Click **Download** button next to file
4. File saves to your device

---

## IMPORTANT: What You CANNOT Do

| Action | Why Not |
|--------|---------|
| Change your marks | Read-only |
| Change attendance | Teacher enters this |
| Upload files | Students can only download |
| Change password | Fixed to your DOB |
| See other students' data | Privacy rules |

---

## Using on Mobile Phone

- The student portal works on phones
- Swipe left/right to see more
- Portrait mode works best
- Tables scroll sideways on small screens

---

## Problems?

| Problem | What to Do |
|---------|-----------|
| Can't login | Check DOB format (DDMMYYYY) |
| Wrong marks showing | Tell your teacher |
| Missing attendance | Tell your teacher |
| App not loading | Check internet, try again |

**Need more help?** → Tell your class teacher or school office
