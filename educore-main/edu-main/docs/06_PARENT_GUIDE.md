## Getting Started

### Your Login Credentials

- **Username**: Your email address (given by school)
- **Password**: Your **child's** date of birth in DDMMYYYY format

**Example:**
- Child born on March 15, 2012
- Your password: `15032012`

**Note:** You use your CHILD's birthday, not your own.

---

## Login Steps

1. Open the school app/website
2. Enter your email
3. Enter your child's date of birth as password
4. Click **Login**
5. You will see the Parent Dashboard

---

## What You Can See

| Section | Information |
|---------|-------------|
| **Child Overview** | Your child's profile |
| **Attendance** | Daily attendance record |
| **Marks** | Exam scores and grades |
| **Fees** | Fee status and payment history |
| **Remarks** | Teacher comments about your child |

---

## View Your Child's Information

### Attendance

1. Click **Attendance** tab
2. See calendar showing:
   - ✓ Green = Present
   - ✗ Red = Absent
   - ⏰ Yellow = Late
3. See overall attendance percentage

### Marks/Grades

1. Click **Marks** tab
2. See all exams:
   - Subject name
   - Exam type
   - Marks scored
   - Grade (A+, A, B+, B, C, F)

### Fee Status

1. Click **Fees** tab
2. See:
   - Total fees
   - Amount paid
   - Amount due
   - Due date
   - Payment history

---

## Multiple Children

If you have more than one child in the school:

1. Your account is linked to all children
2. Use tabs to switch between children
3. Each child's data shown separately

---

## IMPORTANT: What You CANNOT Do

| Action | Reason |
|--------|--------|
| Change any data | View-only access |
| See other students | Privacy rules |
| Contact teacher directly | Use school communication |
| Make payments online | Contact school office |

## Common Questions

### Q: Why can't I log in?
**A:** Make sure you're using your CHILD's birthday (DDMMYYYY format), not your own.

### Q: Why can't I see my child's data?
**A:** Ask the school admin to link your account to your child.

### Q: How do I pay fees?
**A:** Contact the school office. Online payment coming soon.

### Q: Can I message the teacher?
**A:** Use the school's official communication channels.
